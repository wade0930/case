    • Profile

    From the small shoe shop in the Japanese occupation era, the career of a professional started, honing the skills passed down from generation to generation to complying with the digital trend, the courage to try innovation and create new value and strive to develop a new direction, in line with the new mainstream of the times and the value and belief of sustainable management. From the early hand-sewn high-grade gentleman's shoes to the last industry, from the initial hand-made wooden shoe last to the sophisticated multi-axis CNC automatic manufacturing.

    Since its development, there are currently 6 brothers in the third generation, each with at least 45 years of experience, all of whom are senior last masters. There are 15 people in the fourth generation, including more than 21 family members and 35 senior employees of the Taiwan head office. They are rotated in the mainland and Vietnam respectively to maintain the stability of the quality of lasts between factories. In the past 35 years, they have been in Taiwan and the mainland. It is highly recognized by the shoe industry.

    Philosophy

    Since its establishment, it has been committed to providing customers with quality products that are convenient for life, and future generations have continued the original intention and values ​​at the time of entrepreneurship, and insisted that the products produced represent a kind of trust and quality assurance. Since the development of Tuhuolong shoe lasts, we have a team of experienced seniors who continue to absorb foreign advanced technology and introduce high-end equipment, use high-quality plastic materials to improve the quality of the lasts, and work closely with well-known manufacturers and sports brands around the world. Invited to participate in the formulation of advanced Chinese and foreign last standards. And cooperate with a number of new development and production projects, accumulate specific and rich experience, and maintain a certain leading position in the industry in the world. In the future, we will adhere to the original intention of quality requirements, continue to provide customers with high-quality services and technologies, and then achieve product value.
