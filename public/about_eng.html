The family business has changed from shoe manufacturing to the lasts profession, and from the hand-made wood lasts to the machine-made and finally to the precision multi-axis production. All the family members have been joining the business within these 90 years.     Principle

      Since 1930, the family business has changed from shoe manufacturing to the lasts profession, and from the hand-made lasts to the machine-made and finally to the CNC-lasts. Currently, the 3rd generation consists of six brothers, all of whom have over 45 years’ experience. There are ten people in the 4th generation, and a total of 15family members including all the relatives. The 45 skilled staff in the Taiwan head office move to the sites in China and Vietnam as required, to maintain a constant quality of the products from all of our factories. The company’s products for the shoe profession in Taiwan and China have been well received over the last 35 years.

      TWU HUOLONG aims to provide the high-quality products for our customers since the company was found. Keeping the goal as we continue our business, the products we provide needed to be trusted and quality assurance. At present, TWU HUOLONG has developed an experienced team and keep improving our technology, equipment, and using high quality plastic raw material to maintain the quality. By working closely with well-known shoe manufacturers and brands, TWU HUOLONG has been highly trusted and recognized and has a leading position in the world. By having the technology of multi-axis CNC production, we are able to provide customized surface and diversity processing of auto lasts and maintain high precision and ultimate yield.
